Welcome

Friends and Fellow Constituents,

I am excited to formally announce my candidacy for seeking the nomination for the Progressive Conservative Party here in Sherwood Park. I am seeking this opportunity so that I can make a constructive contribution to our community, and our great province. I commit to bringing fresh ideas, energy, and a determination to achieve real results to the legislature and the province as your representative. Communities and the government have talked much over the past couple of years, and now it is time to do something with the information that you as voters have provided.

If ever there was a time when you could make a difference, this is the time. I believe that the PC Party still holds the right values for our province and our community, but that it needs revitalization. The party has been reenergized with the election of our new leader Alison Redford, and I am excited to be a part of that as a PC nominee here in Sherwood Park.

I want to commit to increasing the public’s access to their MLA. By holding open meetings once every four months, members of the public would have a chance to interact with their MLA face-to-face. This would allow an open forum to share concerns, and ideas for making our community and our province better. This grassroots approach will do several things. First, this would increase my accountability to you as your MLA with your ability to personally question me regarding current issues. Second, it will allow me as your MLA to have a better idea what concerns are important to you while representing you in the legislature. And third, by doing this as a PC, I would be able to boost support for our party through increased visibility and by showing that I care about residents’ concerns.

Thank you for taking the time to visit the site. Feel free to navigate around the site to find out more about my vision for the community in which we live. If you have any questions, or would like to volunteer, please feel free to contact myself and my team. I look forward to hearing from you soon.

See you at the door!
